About US

Fountain Shipping Services Limited is an indigenous shipping company that provides a wide range of shipping, logistics and marine services in Nigerian ports and terminals. Incorporated in 2009, Fountain Shipping Services Limited offers top-notch services in the oil and gas industry.

We are fully positioned to meet and exceed customers’ needs for quality and satisfaction be it onshore or offshore. We partner with globally recognized vessel owners and oil traders navigating the West Africa’s territorial waters; our experienced personnel effectively care for our operations to ensure efficiency, quality and professionalism.

Fountain Shipping Services is duly registered with relevant government agencies involved with shipping operations. Our offices are strategically located within Nigeria to serve our customers’ needs.

Fountain Shipping Services Limited has a certified ISO 45001:2018 Occupational Health and Safety management system. Our safety policy is a commitment to the implementation of ISO 45001:2018 which enables us as an organization to provide a safe and healthy work environment.  

  • Our Mission - To provide a world class range of professional quality services in the marine industry to all our clients.
  • Our Vision - To be the foremost shipping company in Africa that is most admired for it's excellence, efficiency and professionalism.
  • Our Core Values - We posses and have displayed varied degree of values which our clients admire viz: Professionalism, Excellence, Integrity, Commitment & Dedication.
